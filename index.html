CRYPTOCURRENCIES OF THE WORLD

Bitcoin

Bitcoin is the first decentralized cryptocurrency. Based on a free-market ideology, bitcoin was invented in 2008 when an unknown entity published a white paper under the pseudonym of Satoshi Nakamoto.

Ethereum

Ethereum is a decentralized blockchain with smart contract functionality. Ether is the native cryptocurrency of the platform. Among cryptocurrencies, ether is second only to bitcoin in market capitalization.

XRP

Created by Ripple in 2012, XRP offers a way to pay in many different real-world currencies.
